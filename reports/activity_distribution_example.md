# Activity Distribution Example

This is an example of the activity distribution histogram generated by the analyzer.

![Distribution of Learning Activities](https://github.com/RoadToDestiny/learning-path-analyzer/raw/main/reports/activity_distribution_example.png)

This visualization shows:
- **Quiz**: 8 activities
- **Assignment**: 8 activities  
- **Forum Post**: 6 activities

The chart clearly displays the distribution of different learning activity types, making it easy to identify which activities are most frequently used in the course.
